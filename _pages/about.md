## About Me

I am a **Senior Vision Algorithm Engineer** at **[Honor](https://www.honor.com/cn/)**, working at the intersection of **optics, sensors, and generative AI** to build the **next generation of AI-native imaging systems** across **device and cloud**. 

I received my Ph.D. from the **[College of Optical Science and Engineering, Zhejiang University](http://opt.zju.edu.cn/)** in June 2025. I was selected as a member of the **inaugural cohort** of the **[Pujiang National Laboratory Joint Training Ph.D. Program (2022–2025)](https://www.shlab.org.cn/news/5443403.html)**, where I conducted my doctoral research under **[Dr. Yueting Chen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=gS-0tfAAAAAJ&hl=en)** and **[Prof. Tianfan Xue](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=RfSQKrIAAAAJ&hl=en)**. 

My work lies at the interface of **optical engineering**, **computational imaging**, and **AI-enhanced vision**, with a particular emphasis on **generative AI for physically grounded imaging and restoration**. I am especially interested in **bridging physical image formation with modern generative models** to enable robust, real-world imaging systems.

I have published in **top-tier venues** such as **AAAI**, **SIGGRAPH Asia**, **IEEE TGRS**, and **Optics Express**, on topics including:

- Degradation modeling and restoration for **remote sensing push-broom / TDI cameras**
- **Event-based low-light frame interpolation** and sim-to-real transfer
- **Atmospheric turbulence mitigation** and **interferometric imaging reconstruction**
- Real-world **motion deblurring** and **AI-ISP** pipelines

These works collectively aim at a coherent agenda: **understanding and inverting complex imaging pipelines with AI, from photons to pixels and beyond**.

I hold a **B.S. in Optoelectronic Information Science and Engineering** from **[Central South University](https://www.csu.edu.cn/)**, where I received the **National Scholarship** and was recognized as an **Outstanding Graduate of Hunan Province**. I then pursued an integrated **Master’s–Ph.D. program** at Zhejiang University, with my Master’s research focused on **computational imaging** under **[Prof. Huajun Feng](https://person.zju.edu.cn/0086127)**.

Throughout my doctoral training, I had the privilege to collaborate with leading experts including **[Shi Guo](https://guoshi28.github.io/)**, **[Zhihai Xu](https://person.zju.edu.cn/0089108)**, **[Jinwei Gu](https://www.gujinwei.org/)**, **[Yihao Liu](https://lyh-18.github.io/)**, **[Xuelong Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ahUibskAAAAJ&hl=zh-TW)**, **[Qi Li](https://person.zju.edu.cn/0098047)**, and **[Bin Zhao](https://iopen.nwpu.edu.cn/info/1251/1852.htm)**, which has shaped my vision for **world-class, interdisciplinary AI imaging research**.

### Vision & Long-term Research Agenda

My long-term goal is to become a **world-leading expert in AI-native imaging systems**, acting as a **system-level architect and scientific leader** at the intersection of:

- **Imaging physics and optical design**
- **Sensor and ISP pipelines**
- **Edge and cloud computation**
- **Generative and foundation models for vision**

Concretely, I am building towards:

- **Hardware–software co-designed imaging systems**  
  Co-optimizing **optics, sensors, ISP, NPU, and generative models** under real-world constraints (noise, motion, low light, bandwidth, power), aiming for **deployable, robust, and upgradable imaging stacks**.

- **Cloud–edge collaborative generative imaging**  
  Designing **end-to-end pipelines** where mobile/edge devices and cloud services jointly perform **restoration, enhancement, synthesis, and editing**, with **learned task partitioning, adaptive quality–latency trade-offs, and strong physical priors**.

- **Physically grounded generative models**  
  Embedding **image formation models, sensor characteristics, and degradation processes** into generative frameworks (e.g., diffusion models) to achieve **controllable, trustworthy, and domain-transferable imaging** in challenging regimes such as **remote sensing, event-based imaging, and interferometric systems**.

- **Scalable, patentable imaging platforms**  
  Developing **reusable architectures, toolchains, and algorithms** that can be instantiated across **consumer devices, remote sensing payloads, and specialized scientific instruments**, with an eye toward **long-term impact, standardization, and IP protection**.

I am particularly excited about collaborations that **span from physical prototypes to large-scale deployments**, and about building **teams and ecosystems** around this vision.

---

### Research Interests

My research and engineering expertise encompass:

- **Generative Artificial Intelligence for imaging**  
  (enhancement, restoration, synthesis, sim-to-real, physically informed generative models)

- **Optical Engineering and Computational Photography**  
  (imaging system modeling, coded and unconventional imaging, interferometric and remote sensing systems)

- **Computer Vision and Graphics**  
  (image/video restoration, enhancement, and content-aware synthesis)

- **Sensor Technologies and Signal Processing**  
  (event-based cameras, push-broom/TDI sensors, imaging under complex degradations)

- **Video and Image Enhancement in the wild**  
  (low-light, motion blur, atmospheric turbulence, real-time and resource-constrained settings)

I am driven by the belief that **the next leap in imaging quality and capability will come from deeply unifying physics-based models, modern AI, and system-level co-design**.
