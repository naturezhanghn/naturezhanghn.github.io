Research Focus in Generative AI & Computational Photography

My research centers on generative models for imaging and computational photography, with an emphasis on physics-informed design and real-world deployment. I work on end-to-end pipelines that connect photon capture, sensor readout, and learned image formation in both consumer and remote sensing systems.

I am particularly interested in combining generative AI with optical and sensor models to achieve robust imaging in challenging environments such as low light, satellite motion jitter, and complex interferometric setups.
